Education in Hollis

The small town vibe of Hollis fosters a highly dedicated and close student body. Throughout my years in the Hollis education system I not only bonded with my peers, but also became extremely close with a lot of my teachers. The teachers were so passionate and really cared about the things they were teaching their students. Success both in and out of the classroom was always celebrated. Opportunities to take AP classes as well as vocational courses at neighboring schools were always available. Personally, I participated in various choir groups, honor societies, rock climbing club, varsity soccer, pep rally skit, ski program, as well as community service hours. These are memories I hold dear to my heart and I know that a lot of it I can give credit to the education I received.
