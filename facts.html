Hollis Main Facts & Demographics

A Brief History...

The town of Hollis was incorporated into the New Hampshire province on April 3,1746. At the time, Governer Benning Wentworth felt he owed the 1st Duke of Newcastle, Thomas Pelham-Holles something in order to repay him for naming him the governor of the town. Thus, the town was originally named Holles. As time passed, both Holles and Hollis were used.
