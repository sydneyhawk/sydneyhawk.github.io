Food in Hollis

Enjoy a delicious breakfast at a cozy diner, order some pizza takeout, or go apple and pumpkin picking! All locations listed are extremely close to the center of town. If you are interested in travelling beyond Hollis, there are plenty of dining options in New Hampshire. I recommend checking out Main Street in Nashua. A family from Hollis owns a restaurant named Surf, it is my dad's personal favorite for any special occasion.

Restaurants

  • Market Place Diner: 4 Market Place
  • Hollis Country Kitchen: 3 Proctor Hill Road
  • Hollis House of Pizza: 22 Ash Street
  • Monument Square Market: 4 Monument Square

Grocery Stores & Fast Food

  • Harvest Market: 2 Market Place
  • Dunkin' Donuts: 2 Main Street

Farms

  • Brookdale Fruit Farm Inc.: 41 Broad Street
  • Lull Farm LLC: 65 Broad Street
  • Lavoies Farm: 172 Nartoff Road
